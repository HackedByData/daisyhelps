A patient voice helper
for your computer.

Daisy listens, looks at your screen when you ask, and walks you through anything — one calm step at a time. She never clicks for you.

Download for Windows — Free

When Windows asks "Are you sure?", click More info then Run anyway. We're working on a signed installer.

What Daisy does

  • 🎙
    Listens to your voice. Just talk — no typing needed.
  • 🖥
    Sees your screen when you ask. Click "Show Daisy my screen" and she'll know exactly what you're looking at.
  • 👣
    Guides one step at a time. No jargon, no rushing.
  • 🌎
    Speaks English or Spanish. Switch with one tap.

Your privacy

Daisy doesn't track you. The app sends your voice and — when you ask — a screen image to our servers to figure out the next step, then forgets the conversation when you close the window.
